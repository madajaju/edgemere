AggregatedDevice

This is a construct that has other devices under it

Attributes

  • name:string - Name of the aggregated device
  • ename:string - Extended name of the aggregated device
  • hostname:string - Hostname of the device
  • type:string - Type of the Device

Methods

  • addDevices() - Add Devices to the Aggregated Device
  • disable() - Disable Device and its hardware
  • enable() - Enable Device to be used.
  • reserve() - Reserve a resource
  • addToHardware() - Create a Device

  • getReservations() - Get reservation from the requirements
  • provision() - Provision Hardware to meet the requirements
